Remarks
"Code Separated Value" is supposed to be a generalization of "Comma Separated Value" with a conveniently identical abbreviation. The terms "Record Separator" and "Unit Separator" refer to the ASCII control codes (0x30 and 0x31 respectively) which were originally defined for the purpose of encoding CSV-type data. The Wikipedia-sanctioned term is "Separator-Separated-Values".
